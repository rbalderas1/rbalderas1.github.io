Located on University Boulevard, just West of the University of Arizona, Wildcat Restaurant will provide students and Tucsonians alike with ready access to delicious, flame grilled dishes starting in December, 2021.

Story

We are Peggy Smith and Michael Brown, two chefs and lovers of all things grilled. We wanted to share our passion for grilling with residents of Tucson, so we started Wildcat Restaurant. While the Coronavirus pandemic certainly made starting a business difficult, we are on track to open in December 2021.

We hope very much that you can come visit us!

Mission

Our mission is to share with our customers the rich flavors of a flame grilled meal. Whether you're a meat lover or vegetarian, we have plenty of dishes we are sure you will enjoy.
